# Fit-Nation: The Brutally Honest Fitness Engine 

Fit-Nation is a logic-driven fitness analyzer that cuts through the noise of the fitness industry to provide you with raw, honest, and actionable training advice based on your current life constraints.

## ‚öôÔ∏è How it works?
The engine uses a deterministic **Rule-Based Analysis** system:
1. **Data Collection:** You input your age, weight, height, sleep, activity, time, and consistency patterns.
2. **BMI & Category:** It calculates your body metrics to understand your physical starting point.
3. **Bottleneck Identification:** It compares your goals against your lifestyle (e.g., "Muscle Gain" vs "5 hours of sleep").
4. **Logic Trace:** It runs your data through a series of "if-then" fitness principles (e.g., *If sleep < 6 hours, then High-Intensity Gym is high-risk*).
5. **Path Selection:** It determines which training styles (Gym, Home, HIIT, Walking) are currently safe and effective for you.
